About me

Hi! I am a first-year PhD student in Management Science and Operations at London Business School. I completed my B.Sc. in Industrial Engineering at Sharif University of Technology, followed by an M.Sc. in Industrial Engineering with a specialization in Optimization Methods at the same institution. My previous research has been published in journals such as Computers & Operations Research, and I am currently exploring various topics in Operations Management.
